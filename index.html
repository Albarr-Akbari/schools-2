welcome to blackwood primary school

Blackwood Primary School is a great place for children to learn, we have an IB School and we do our best to achieve care, harmony, and exellence

Campus Life

The Hard Courts

This is where we play handball and basketball, huge space and great for chasey.

The library

The library is a place with all types of books, they are: Picture books, non-fiction, fiction, YA (Young Adults for year 6 only) and junior fiction. They also have computers to help find books.

music and drama

This is where we teach kids music and some acting along with some fun games ever now and again.

The oval

This is the oval, it has a cricket pitch, soccer goals and cricket nets at the back. Every year we have a sport competition called Blue Earth Day with 4 teams: Bradman(green), Thomson(Blue), Fraser(yellow) and Rosewall(Red)

The hall

The hall is where we have Physical Education(PE) and a before and after school care for parents that have a job and need to drop off or pick up their kids early or late from 7am to 5pm

Back Courts

We also have back courts with handball, basketball, and netball.
